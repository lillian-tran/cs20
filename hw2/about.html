about us:

Rise Young is a youth empowerment nonprofit that ensures that underprivileged young people get the resources they need in order to succeed. We work with kids as young as 5 up until they turn 19, because we believe in the value of starting early. Our services include:

  1. additional tutoring personalized to the individual to ensure they stay on track with their peers
  2. a mentorship program that matches people with a successful professional in their field of choice
  3. career and educational guidance to set a young person up for their definition of success
  4. policy advocacy to ensure that youth voices are being heard and we are creating an equitable environment for them
  5. and so much more!

Our application for the coming year has already been closed, but feel free to watch this space for any updates, or reach out to us to discuss if Rise Young will be a good fit for you. If you are under 18, please have your parent reach out to us.
